Machine Editor

Design your processes visually

The Machine Editor lets you design your machine's complete control sequence. Drag and connect valves, pumps, tanks, sensors, filters, membranes, mixers and filling machinery to create custom process flows.

  • Configurable Setpoints

    Each sensor has a modifiable setpoint that defines operation thresholds and triggers automatic actions

  • Nodes & Dependencies

    Create logical nodes between sensors to build complex process actions and sequences with dependencies

  • Multi-Step Sequences

    Define backwash, CIP cleaning, filling and more sequences with timed steps and sensor conditions

  • Industrial Equipment

    Support for valves, pumps, tanks, sensors, filters, membranes, mixers and filling machinery

Web Portal for Distributors

Manage your entire operation from a single place

  • Multi-Client & Multi-Machine Management

    Manage different clients and different machines from the same place. Unified dashboard with real-time status of your entire fleet

  • Remote Monitoring & Control

    Monitor sensors, water quality and equipment status in real time. Control treatment plants remotely without on-site visits

  • Maintenance & Service Scheduling

    Manage maintenance services, review upcoming scheduled maintenance, parts and spares management, and complete service history per machine

  • Technician & Route Management in Real Time

    Assign routes to your technicians, track their location in real time, manage their workload and optimize field service routes

  • Trends, Alerts & Webhook Logs

Mobile App for Clients

Your clients connected to their machines

The Vontech app empowers your end clients with direct access to their machine status, real-time water quality, technician tracking and direct communication with the manufacturer or distributor.

  • Machine Status & Water Quality

    Clients see their equipment status in real time, TDS readings, conductivity, pressure, temperature and all sensors. Control available in advanced versions

  • Technician & Service Tracking

    Clients can track their assigned technician live and follow the status of their maintenance service in real time

  • Service History & Warranties

    Access to complete history of services performed, warranty management and documentation for each maintenance

  • Requests & Direct Communication

    Clients can request any service and communicate directly with the manufacturer or distributor from the app, no middlemen
