The Problem

Why water plants are moving away from SCADA

Traditional SCADA systems were designed for large-scale industrial plants. For water treatment, they bring unnecessary complexity and cost.

Expensive Licenses

SCADA software licenses cost thousands per year. Add PLC programming fees, server hardware, and IT maintenance. Total cost of ownership keeps growing.

Requires Programming

Traditional SCADA needs PLC ladder logic, HMI screen development, and database configuration. Changes require hiring an integrator every time.

Slow Deployment

A traditional SCADA project takes weeks or months: system design, PLC programming, HMI development, testing, and commissioning. Guardian HMI deploys in days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about replacing SCADA

What is the difference between Guardian HMI and traditional SCADA?

Traditional SCADA requires a PLC, separate HMI software, a SCADA server, licensed software, and programming expertise. Guardian HMI is an all-in-one industrial touchscreen with built-in controller, IoT connectivity, web portal, and mobile app — no programming, no license fees, no separate server.

Can Guardian HMI replace my existing SCADA system?

Yes. Guardian HMI can replace traditional SCADA in water treatment applications including reverse osmosis, ultrafiltration, water softeners, media filters, and filling machines. It provides real-time monitoring, remote access, alarm management, and historical data logging.

How much does Guardian HMI cost compared to SCADA?

Guardian HMI costs a fraction of a traditional SCADA system. There are no annual software license fees, no PLC programming costs, and no separate server hardware required. Contact us for a custom quote based on your application.

Does Guardian HMI support remote monitoring?

Yes. Guardian HMI includes built-in WiFi and connects to the Vontech web portal and mobile app. Monitor your entire fleet of water treatment systems from anywhere in the world, receive push notifications, and control equipment remotely.

What water treatment applications does it support?

Guardian HMI is designed for reverse osmosis (RO), ultrafiltration (UF), water softeners, media filters (carbon, sand, multimedia), and bottle/jug filling machines. Each application comes with pre-built P&ID diagrams and control logic.
