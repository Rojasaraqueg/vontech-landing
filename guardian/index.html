Visual Process Designer

Design your water treatment processes visually

The Machine Editor lets you design your water treatment machine's complete control sequence. Drag and connect valves, pumps, tanks, sensors, filters, membranes, mixers and filling machinery to create custom process flows for RO, UF, softeners and more.

  • Configurable Setpoints

    Each sensor has a modifiable setpoint that defines operation thresholds and triggers automatic actions for water quality control

  • Nodes & Dependencies

    Create logical nodes between sensors to build complex water treatment sequences with dependencies

  • Multi-Step Sequences

    Define backwash, CIP cleaning, filling and regeneration sequences with timed steps and sensor conditions

  • Industrial Equipment Library

    Built-in support for valves, pumps, tanks, sensors, RO membranes, media filters, softener resins and filling machinery

HMI Controller Features

Everything you need to automate water treatment

A complete ecosystem of tools designed to maximize the efficiency and reliability of your water treatment processes.

Real-Time Water Quality Monitoring

Live readings of TDS, conductivity, pressure, temperature, pH, ORP, resistivity and customizable sensor configurations.

Automated Task Scheduling

Schedule backwash, regeneration, readings and maintenance on automatic schedules with weekly, monthly or yearly frequency.

Alarm & Alert System

Configurable threshold-based alarms with detailed history, push notifications and automatic escalation for water quality deviations.

Valve Control with P&ID

Open and close with touch confirmation, interactive P&ID process diagram and real-time visual status feedback.

User & Role Management

Role-based access control with security classification. Administrator, operator and viewer with granular permissions.

Historical Trends & Analytics

Interactive graphs with 2h, 24h, 7d zoom and custom ranges for deep water quality data analysis and compliance reporting.

Activity Log & Audit Trail

Complete record of every action with timestamp, user and description. Full operations traceability for compliance.

Dark & Light Theme

Choose between dark theme for control rooms or light theme based on your preference and lighting conditions.

WiFi IoT Connectivity

Built-in WiFi for real-time sync between HMI, cloud and mobile devices with secure encrypted protocol.

  • Maintenance & Service Scheduling

Mobile Water Monitoring App

Your clients connected to their water systems

The Vontech mobile app empowers your end clients with direct access to their water treatment system status, real-time water quality data, technician tracking and direct communication with the manufacturer or distributor.

  • Real-Time Water Quality Data

    Clients see their equipment status, TDS, conductivity, pressure, temperature and all sensor readings in real time

  • Technician & Service Tracking

    Clients can track their assigned technician live and follow the status of their maintenance service in real time

  • Alerts & Notifications

    Instant push notifications for water quality deviations, maintenance reminders and system status changes

  • Service Requests & Direct Communication

    Clients can request services and communicate directly with the manufacturer or distributor from the app
