The Problem

Why softeners need smart controllers

Traditional softener controllers use basic timers and lack real-time monitoring, leading to waste and inconsistent water quality.

Timer-Based Regeneration

Regenerating on a fixed schedule wastes salt and water. Demand-based regeneration triggered by actual hardness readings is far more efficient.

No Remote Visibility

Without remote monitoring, hardness breakthroughs and regeneration failures go undetected until downstream equipment is damaged.

Duplex Coordination

Managing duplex or triplex softener alternation manually is complex. Automatic valve sequencing ensures continuous soft water supply.

Softener Controller Features

Everything you need to automate your softener

Hardness Monitoring

Real-time inlet and outlet hardness tracking with configurable alarm thresholds to detect breakthroughs instantly.

Automated Regeneration

Demand-based or scheduled regeneration with multi-step sequences: backwash, brine draw, slow rinse, fast rinse and service.

Duplex Alternation

Automatic alternation between tanks in duplex systems. One tank always in service while the other regenerates for 24/7 soft water.

Remote Monitoring

Monitor all your softener systems from anywhere via the web portal. Track regeneration history, salt usage, and water quality remotely.

Salt & Efficiency Tracking

Monitor salt consumption per regeneration, track efficiency over time, and optimize brine dosing to reduce operating costs.

Alarm System

Hardness breakthrough alerts, regeneration failure notifications, low salt warnings, and flow anomaly detection with push notifications.
