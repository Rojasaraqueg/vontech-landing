The Problem

Why RO plants need smart controllers

Traditional RO systems rely on manual monitoring and basic PLCs, leading to costly problems.

Membrane Damage

Without real-time pressure and TDS monitoring, membranes degrade faster. Replacing RO membranes is expensive and causes downtime.

Manual Monitoring

On-site visits to check readings waste time and resources. Problems go undetected until they become critical failures.

Inconsistent Water Quality

Without automated alerts for TDS spikes or pressure drops, permeate quality fluctuates and can fail compliance requirements.
