Industrial Solutions

Industrial-Grade Water Treatment

Our industrial product line is engineered for demanding environments. From reverse osmosis plants handling 50,000+ GPD to multi-tank media filter batteries, every system is built with industrial-grade materials, precision valves, and integrated smart controls.

  • Reverse Osmosis Plants — 500 to 50,000+ GPD, multi-membrane, auto CIP/backwash
  • Media Filter Batteries — Sand, carbon, multimedia in steel pressure vessels
  • Duplex Softener Systems — Continuous soft water with alternating FRP tanks
  • Filling Machines — Automated filling stations with robotic arm and PLC
  • UV & Chemical Dosing — Complete post-treatment and disinfection
Filling Systems

Automated Filling Machines

Our automated filling stations handle everything from IBC totes to individual bottles. Equipped with a precision robotic arm, PLC-controlled dispensing, level sensors, and integrated control panels — designed for water production and distribution facilities.

  • Robotic Arm Dispensing — Precision XY gantry system for automated filling
  • PLC Control Panel — Programmable logic with touchscreen interface
  • IBC & Bottle Compatible — Handles totes, drums, and bottle containers
  • Storage Tank Integration — Connected to large-capacity storage with level monitoring
Residential Solutions

Clean Water for Every Home

Our residential product line brings industrial-grade water treatment technology to homes and small businesses. FRP pressure vessels with programmable digital valve heads for softening, carbon filtration, sediment removal, and iron treatment.

  • Water Softeners — Ion exchange resin with automatic brine regeneration
  • Carbon Filters — Activated carbon for chlorine, taste, and odor removal
  • Sediment Filters — Multi-media for particulate and turbidity removal
  • Digital Valve Heads — Programmable timer or meter-based regeneration
  • Cartridge Housings — 10", 20", Big Blue for point-of-use filtration

Guardian HMI — Industrial Touchscreen

The Guardian HMI is an industrial touchscreen available in 13", 15", 21" and 32" sizes with interactive P&ID diagrams, real-time sensor monitoring, valve control, alarm management, and complete process automation. It can be integrated into any existing or new machine.

Interactive P&ID — Tap valves and pumps on the process flow
Customizable Sensors — TDS, conductivity, pH, pressure, temperature, ORP and more
Configurable Alarms — Per-sensor thresholds with history
Scheduled Tasks — Automated backwash, CIP, sampling
User Roles — Admin, Operator, Viewer permissions
Activity Log — Complete audit trail
